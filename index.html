The color-blind simulator and contrast checker for Sketch. Design with accessibility in mind.

Check your designs directly in Sketch

Using the preview window, select from the simulator and contract checker to ensure your design is as great for those who don’t see the way a typical person does.

Select from 8 color profiles

Simulate the various forms of color-blindness by quickly selecting and adjusting your designs accordingly.

Integrated contrast checker

Ensure your primary, secondary and typography work hand in hand, reaching the necessary level to be as readable and soft on the eyes as possible.
